Second Self - Band identity

Modern Constructivism

Second Self are an alternative quartet band based in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire, I approached this band with a branding proposal to reinvent their image. Working closely alongisde them I designed their new identity and a short lyric animation video.

Second Self are an alternative quartet band based in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire, I approached this band with a branding proposal to reinvent their image. Working closely alongisde them I designed their new identity and a short lyric animation video.

Ideas.

After an initial meeting with the band we discussed some general ideas and sketches which they provided. I proceeded to create a variety of logo designs to better judge their taste for style as a distinct design choice was not specified this early.

Development.

After several discussions with the band we decided on a general style to suit their needs. This style was then taken further and developed into multiple variations to allow them another range of designs to choose from.

Mock up.

A final design was chosen by the band and I worked on creating some mock up designs to portray their new identity in context to them.

Completion.

With the final design chosen and approved with the band I then began creating many mock up designs such as posters, vinyl cover, shirts and even an animation. Once compelte, the band was then send the many files formatted for all their needs.
