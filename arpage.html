Antibiotic Resistance

As part of a small team at Gloucester College we were approached by Gloucester City Council to design an awareness campaign regarding the issue of antibiotic resistance.

As part of a small team at Gloucester College we were approached by Gloucester City Council to design an awareness campaign regarding the issue of antibiotic resistance.

The brief.

Using graphics to raise awareness of antibiotic resistance, with a target audience of 16-24 year old non gender specific people in the Gloucestershire area set by the Gloucestershire County Council.

Development.

Defining key points and the graphics we will use to portray them we then divided these areas amongst our team. Collaborating ideas and designs to produce out final products.

Results.

These poster designs were created alongside a short animation retaining the same themes as the posters, the posters were then displayed on billboards and buses in and around the Gloucestershire area.
