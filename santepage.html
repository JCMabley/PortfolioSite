Santé - Packaging

Creating and producing mock up designs for a beauty brand. A logo, brand name and identity along with packaging for a range of products aimed towards a gender neurtal market and target audience.

Creating and producing mock up designs for a beauty brand. A logo, brand name and identity along with packaging for a range of products aimed towards a gender neurtal market and target audience.

Brand ideas.

Starting my idea generation by brainstorming multiple areas consider health and cosmetics. From this I then chose a handful of words to translate into somethign that sounded more pleasing.

Development.

Once the brand name was settled upon I then proceeded to test and compare multiple typefaces and fonts along with colours. The colours finally chosen were due to their natural feel, environmentally inspired green alongside purity and cleanliness of white.

Mock up.

With the brand identity complete I then worked on a short tagline, something catchy and memorable. 'Feel Good' worked perfectly, short, sweet and simple. I then moved onto the products in which I would design under this new brand name.

Completion.

I decide on a variety of different packagaing ideas, all of them ensuring sustainability and and environmentally friendly materials within their design. These designs were then created in mock up form and the brand design was added along with all other legal information, instructions or warnings.
